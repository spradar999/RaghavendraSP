About Me

I'm a research scholar and interdisciplinary enthusiast working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and remote sensing. My work focuses on crop classification using ViT models, DSSAT-based crop growth simulation, Sentinel-2 spectral analysis for geological mapping, and practical tools for disaster analysis and water management.

I enjoy fieldwork, coding in Google Earth Engine, and developing reproducible research tools that help decision-makers and communities.
